***Spotify Global Music Analytics Dashboard***
**Description**
-Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Spotify music trends, artist performance, popularity behavior, genre dominance and diversity , and listener engagement insights from over 7900+ Track and 2500+ Unique Artists 
**Dataset source**
-Kaggle -Spotify Global Music Dataset (2009-2025)

**TOOLS USED**
-Power Bi
-Power Query
-Dax
-Data cleaning & Transformation

**KPI Highlights**
-Total Tracks
-Number of Unique Artists
-Top followed artists
-Average Track Popularity

**Key Visuals**
-Tracks Trend Over Time
-Popularity Trend
-Genre Distribution 
-Top Artists
-Top Tracks
-Explicit VS Non-Explicit Distribution

**Business Insights Summary**
-Spotify continues to grow with a diverse collection of tracks, artists, releases.

-Engagement is strong with many tracks achieving solid popularity across different years.

-A smaller group of artists still lead the platform, showing consistency and strong fan loyalty.

-Newer songs tend to perform better, suggesting listeners enjoy fresh and trending music.

-Artist popularity and follower count strongly influence track success.

-Overall, Spotify reflects a competitive music environment where standout artists and trending songs drive most engagement.

-The number of tracks released has increased over the years, showing continuous growth in music production.

-Average popularity generally improves over time, meaning newer music is reaching audiences effectively.

-A few key genres dominate listening behavior, attracting most of the engagement.

-Listener preferences are clearly focused rather than evenly spread across all genres.

-These trends highlight how Spotify is shaped by evolving tastes, trending styles, and changing audience interests.

**Final Conclusion**
-Spotify’s ecosystem shows strong growth in track releases over years, with listener engagement increasingly concentrated around highly followed and influential artists. 

-Popularity trends indicate that newer tracks generally perform better, reflecting evolving listener preferences and the rise of trending music.

-Genres are not evenly consumed, but a few dominant genres capture most of the audience’s attention, highlighting focused listening behavior. 

-Overall, Spotify remains a highly dynamic platform driven by strong artist influence, evolving music trends, and audience loyalty toward leading performers.

**How This Dashboard Helps Businesses**

This Power BI dashboard provides data-driven insights that help music platforms, record labels, marketers, and business teams make informed strategic decisions.

1-Streaming Platforms (Spotify / Apple Music / Anghami / Deezer):

-Identify trending years of music performance and shifts in popularity.

-Track popularity trends over time to understand listener behavior.

-Analyze explicit vs non-explicit content performance to support audience content strategy.

-Monitor catalog health by understanding how many tracks and artists are active.

2-Record Labels & Artist Management:

-Discover top-performing artists and understand what drives their success.

-Identify high-performing tracks to prioritize marketing and promotion spending.

-Evaluate genre performance to guide signing strategy and investment decisions.

-Benchmark new or emerging artists against top performers

3-Business & Strategy Teams:

-Support data-driven strategic planning using historic trends.

-Understand listener preferences based on genre dominance and popularity behavior.

-Prioritize business opportunities in high-growth music segments.

-Optimize content budgets based on what consistently performs well.

4-Marketing & Campaign Teams:

-Target the right artists and genres for promotional campaigns.

-Design audience-specific marketing strategies (e.g., explicit content vs family-friendly).

-Focus campaigns around high-engagement periods and music eras.

-Improve ROI by aligning campaigns with data-backed insights.
